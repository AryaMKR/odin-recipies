Lasagna

Description

Make a delicious lasagna with a handful of ingredients!

Ingredients
  • pasta plates
  • tomato
  • shredded mozarella cheese
  • ground beef
  • spices
  • love
Steps
  1. Turn on the oven
  2. brown ground beef in a frying pann
  3. blend tomato in with the beef
  4. Put the beef blend and pasta plates into a form in layers
  5. finish the layers with a lot of mozarella cheese
  6. cook in the oven until the cheese is browned
  7. Serve with sour cream and love. Enjoy!
